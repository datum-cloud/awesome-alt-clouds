About / Philosophy

Why Alternative Clouds Matter

The cloud landscape has long been dominated by a few hyperscalers. While they offer incredible breadth, many developers find themselves paying for complexity they don't need, or missing specialized capabilities that could transform their applications.

Alternative cloud providers fill these gaps. They offer focused expertise, transparent pricing, and often better economics for specific workloads. A GPU cloud built by ML engineers understands your training needs. An edge provider knows latency like the hyperscalers never will.

We believe in a diverse cloud ecosystem where the best tool for the job isn't always the biggest. This directory exists to help you discover these alternatives and make informed choices.

Inclusion Criteria Explained

Every service in our directory must meet at least 2 of 3 criteria:

Public Pricing Self-Service Signup Status Page / SLA
  • Public Pricing: You can see what you'll pay before signing up. No "contact sales" required to understand costs.
  • Self-Service Signup: You can start using the service today without waiting for approval or scheduling demos.
  • Status Page / SLA: The provider publicly commits to uptime and transparency about incidents.

Services meeting all 3 criteria get a green badge (3/3). Those meeting 2 get a yellow badge (2/3). This helps you quickly identify the most developer-friendly options.

Community Guidelines

  • Be helpful: Share your experiences with services, both positive and negative, to help others make informed decisions.
  • Stay objective: Avoid promotional content. We evaluate services on their merits, not marketing.
  • Contribute thoughtfully: When suggesting new services, provide accurate information and real-world context.
  • Respect the ecosystem: Alternative doesn't mean adversarial. Many providers serve different needs than hyperscalers.
